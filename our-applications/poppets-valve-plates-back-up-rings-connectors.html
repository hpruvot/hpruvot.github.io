Connectors

With the “easy” oil and natural gas reserves already tapped, the energy industry has focused on finding oil and gas in many challenging production zones. These tougher to develop wells can expose sensitive electrical equipment and components to increased pressures, temperatures and aggressive chemistries. The electrical connectors in this equipment have to meet these tough demands and Mitsubishi Chemical Advanced Materials can support the thesedemands with engineering support,high-performance polymers development, high-performance thermoplastic shapes, machining services and injection molding services.

As the traditional glass-reinforced thermosets used in connectors have taken a beating exhibiting cracking, de-lamination and chipping in these demanding applications, alternative materials are in demand. With the full line of Mitsubishi Chemical Advanced Materials, available to industry engineers, Ketron PEEK, Duratron PEI, Techtron PPS, Duratron PAI and Semitron offer engineering solutions.

Properties offered:

  • High Mechanical Properties Retention
  • High Dielectric and Dielectric Control
  • Options-Great Solder Reflow Resistance
  • Extremely Low Moisture Absorption
  • Very Good Chemical Resistance
  • Excellent Abrasion Resistance-Stock Shapes
  • Standard Resins and Custom Compounded Resins Available
