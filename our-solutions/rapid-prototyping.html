Rapid Prototyping for Additive Manufacturing, CNC Machining & Injection Molding

Mitsubishi Chemical Advanced Materials is a leader in Engineered Solutions for the Oil & Gas industry. We strive to give the best customer service possible when designing and creating finished parts. Starting from the design phase with the customer, we engage in material selection, rapid prototyping to production and assembly.

For our rapid prototyping capabilities, we offer unique capabilities that allows us to provide both 3D printed type parts and/or CNC machined parts.

  • Our 3D capabilities are unique because we are able to get complex designed parts, with superior mechanical properties, quicker to our customers than our competition.
  • For CNC machined parts we have automated capabilities with feature recognition programming to reduce manufacturing time.
  • Proprietary Additive Manufacturing Technology gives our customer 3D printed dimensional features, but with injection molded mechanical properties. It solves the weakness of current 3D technology printed parts.
