Mitsubishi Chemical Advanced Materials was the world’s FIRST to earn NORSOK M-710 compliance and provides the most accurate and reliable data on their stock shapes.The NORSOK M-710 specification was developed by the Norwegian petroleum industry to ensure sufficient understanding and experience of oil and gas materials. It specifies a test procedure to predict the progressive degradation of thermoplastic materials exposed to a sour fluid at elevated pressure and temperature over an extended period of time.
